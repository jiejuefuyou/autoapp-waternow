# Privacy Policy for WaterNow

**Last updated**: 2026-05-17
**Developer**: Hao Sun (jiejuefuyou@gmail.com)
**Effective for**: WaterNow iOS app, v1.0+

## What we collect

**Nothing.** WaterNow is a 100% offline app. We collect zero information about you, your hydration habits, your device, or your usage patterns.

## Local storage only

Your hydration entries (water log, beverage type, cup size, daily goal) are stored **only on your device**, using iOS's encrypted standard `UserDefaults`.

- We **never** transmit this data anywhere.
- We **never** sync it across devices (no cloud, no server, no iCloud).
- If you delete the app, all data is permanently gone (no recovery possible).

## Third-party services

None. WaterNow does not include any third-party SDKs (no Firebase, no Crashlytics, no analytics, no ads).

The only network call is when you tap **Restore Purchases** or **Buy Pro** — this contacts Apple's StoreKit servers per Apple's iOS payment processing. Apple's privacy policy applies: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

## HealthKit

WaterNow v1.0 does **not** use HealthKit. We do not read from or write to the Apple Health app. (A future Pro tier may add optional HealthKit hydration export — when that ships, this section will be updated and the app will request explicit permission per iOS standard prompts.)

## Push notifications

WaterNow v1.0 does not send remote notifications. Optional **local** hydration reminders may be scheduled by the user on-device (no network involved; iOS handles them locally). These can be disabled in iOS Settings → Notifications → WaterNow.

## App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

We do not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies. Per Apple's ATT requirement, this app does not invoke the ATT permission prompt because we have nothing to track.

## Children

This app does not target users under 13. It is rated 4+ in App Store Connect because it contains no objectionable content.

## Your rights

Since we collect nothing, there is no data to access, correct, or delete. If you want to remove all your hydration entries, simply delete the app, or use the Reset action in Settings.

## Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we'll update the "Last updated" date above. Material changes will be noted in app release notes.
